About me

Hi, I’m Meadin Menbere, an aspiring front-end developer from Ethiopia, now based in the United States. My journey began with designing posters and flyers using Canva for a club I started at school. Feeling limited by its design constraints, I delved into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create more personalized designs. What started as a quest for creative freedom quickly became a passion for web development. Inspired by my Ethiopian heritage and the vibrant tech community here, I focus on building responsive, user-friendly websites that engage and connect users. I also teach myself by joining various worldwide online courses and exploring new technologies to continuously enhance my skills. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my work and reach out if you’d like to collaborate or chat about web development!
